Here are some films that

might interest you.

HER

This film explores the relationship between a man and an

artificial intelligence embodied by a voice through

his device. Communication through technology, human

emotions, and loneliness are at the heart of this story.

Disconnect

This film tells several interconnected stories

about the dangers of the Internet and technology.

It explores how modern means of

communication affect people's lives, sometimes in

tragic ways.

the social

dilemma

A documentary analyzing the impact of social

media and technology on society and human

psychology. This film explores how our phones

and communication platforms influence our behavior.

Transcendence

This film deals with artificial intelligence and the

rise of technology in human life. It explores the fusion

between humans and technology.

Phone Booth

This thriller takes place almost entirely in a

phone booth, where a man is held hostage

by an unseen sniper threatening him over the

phone. The film explores the role of the phone

in a tense situation, playing on psychology and

suspense.

Jobs

This biopic about Steve Jobs tells the story of the

Apple creator and explores the revolutionary impact

of innovations in telephony and computing on modern

society. It highlights the central role of the phone in

transforming communications.

The Matrix

Although this film is more science fiction-oriented,

The Matrix uses the phone as a link between the

virtual and real worlds. Similar to Black Mirror,

it explores how technology can be both a tool

of control and liberation.

Sorry We

Missed You

This film addresses the impact of new communication

technologies on the lives of precarious workers.

Phones and digital applications are at the heart

of employee control, a theme that resonates

with technological and social issues.

The Circle

This film explores the dangers of technological

surveillance through a futuristic society where

phones and social media enable complete

transparency. It addresses the loss of privacy

and the social consequences of technology.

Searching

This thriller follows a father investigating the

disappearance of his daughter using digital tools

and phones. The film unfolds almost entirely

through computer screens, highlighting how

technology and telecommunications play an

essential role in our modern lives.
