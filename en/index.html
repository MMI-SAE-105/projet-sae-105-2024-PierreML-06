Welcome to the exploration

of the Prehistory of the Future

This site invites you to dive into the evolution of

telephony and media, revealing how these

elements have shaped our modern world.

Through five captivating articles, we will first explore

the history of the telephone, before delving into the

film Black Phone, a psychological thriller about

fear and communication.

We will also examine the book "5G for Dummies," which demystifies

5G technology, as well as the song "Carmen" by Stromae, which critiques

human relationships in the age of social networks.

Finally, we will highlight the work of director

Scott Derrickson, whose films question

contemporary fears.

This exploration aims to offer unique perspectives

on our past and provoke thoughts about the future

that awaits us.
