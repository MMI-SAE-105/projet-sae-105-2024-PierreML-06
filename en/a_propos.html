About

Learn more about me

My name is Pierre Mouilleseaux Lhuillier,

I am 18 years old and I am a first-year student

in the Bachelor's degree in Multimedia and

Internet Professions (MMI). Passionate about the evolution

of technologies and their influence on our lives,

I created this website as part of a personal project

called "The Prehistory of the Future on the Phone".

Through this project, I want to invite you to

discover an original reflection on the origins

and the future of our connected devices,

between the past and the future.
